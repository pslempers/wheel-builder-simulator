About the Virtual Wheel Builder

This application will show step-by-step how to build a bicycle wheel.

I'm Peter, the author of this app.

I built thousands of wheels for Santa Cruz and Cervélo bicycles,

and wanted to share what I learned.

My goal was to get the app working with Three.js and make it as interactive as possible to share this knowledge!
